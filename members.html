Research Members

Dr. Davide Lombardi

Dr. Lombardi is a chartered architect and scholar with a Ph.D. in Architecture. His research and professional career span Italy, China, and the UK, focusing on computational tools, blockchain technologies in AEC, and robotic fabrication. As Head of the Department of Architecture at XJTLU, he leads interdisciplinary projects and international collaborations.

Dr. Theodoros Dounas

Dr. Dounas is a registered architect and Web3 engineer with expertise in generative design, AI, and blockchain. He serves as vice-chair of the European Council on Computing in Construction and contributes to decentralized fabrication systems and smart contract applications in AEC.

Dr. Jiří Vele

Dr. Vele is an architect and researcher in 3D concrete printing and robotic fabrication. Co-founder of Scoolpt and ArchiDAO, he explores digital construction workflows, algorithmic slicing, and decentralized collaboration in architectural production.

Mr. Giancarlo Di Marco

Mr. Di Marco is a Senior Associate Professor and Architectural Engineer focusing on intelligent automation in construction through 3DCP and AI-driven adaptive control. He bridges academia and industry to promote innovation in digital fabrication and sustainable design.

Mr. Hico McDonald

Mr. McDonald is a Civil and Web3 Engineer integrating civil engineering expertise with blockchain technology. With a decade of project management experience in South Africa and Thailand, he now works in Web3 partnership management and mentors future blockchain developers.
